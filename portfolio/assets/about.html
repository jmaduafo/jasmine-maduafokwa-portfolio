ABOUT

I’m Jasmine Maduafokwa, a Nigerian-American specializing in both frontend and backend development as well as design. My dream to become a web developer began during COVID, and since then, I’ve been juggling completing my bachelors in science and sneaking in coding and design practice when possible.

As a recent college graduate, I sought to expand my knowledge and skill in coding, using my design instincts to create aesthetically-pleasing, user-focused websites and apps, not just by practicing hours on end independently but also by working in teams, study groups, and learning from other people from various walks of life.
